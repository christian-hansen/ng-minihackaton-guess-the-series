GUESS THE SERIES
RULES

Guess the correct series to gather points.

When the game starts you get the episode's title together with it's season and season episode number as well as the episode's genre and release date.

Use hints

If you need an additional hint you can get the episode's plot displayed by clicking on the button. If you need a second and final hint you can also display an episode image.

Points

If you guess the correct series without additional hints you will be rewarded with 5 points. If you need the plot you still get 2 points. If you guess right after reading the plot and seeing the image you still get 1 point added to your score."

Enjoy playing the game
